Secret Santa
Features
  • Define Partners that should not get eachother
  • Each person can create WISHLISTS
  • Your information (the site requires only email addresses) is kept secure and is not shared
  • After the date of your secret santa has passed, all of the data related to your secret santa and it's group, will be permenantly deleted to increase security
  • Users of your group will recieve an email of which they can access their own wishlist, as well as their secret santa's wishlist. They will not be required to login
Why use this site? I created this site as a project at home, it's free to use (with ads to support hosting costs). I tried to incorporate the core requirements for a secret santa application, but if I have missed any, please contact me and I will see if I can incorporate more!
